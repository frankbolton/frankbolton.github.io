  • Python Flask and HTML5 for generating web based HCI experiments
  • Amazon Mechanical Turk for recruiting experiment participants
  • R for data analysis, statistical testing and generating graphs
  • Latex for paper writing
  • Arduino and Processing for Ubicomp prototyping
I came to Israel to do a PhD, having been awarded a prestigious fellowship as part of a European Commission Framework Program 7 project called iCareNet.
